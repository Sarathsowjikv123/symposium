About US

Unleash Your Tech Potential at Prayuddha '25! ⚔️

Welcome to the Department of Information Technology at UCE - BIT Campus Trichy!


What to Expect?

  • Epic Competitions: Dive into a series of thrilling contests that challenge your coding prowess, innovative thinking, and problem-solving skills. From hackathons to coding challenges, the arena is set for the fiercest tech warriors!
  • Skill Development: As Will Smith said, "Talent you have naturally, Skill is only developed by hours and hours of beating your own craft." Join workshops and tech talks to sharpen your skills and gain insights from industry experts.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow tech enthusiasts, industry leaders, and mentors. Forge alliances and create lasting connections that can propel your career forward.
  • Beyond Tech: Not just for coders! Participate in non-tech events that ignite creativity, teamwork, and strategic thinking. Showcase your versatility and prove your mettle in every arena.
Why Participate? ▶

Prayuddha '25 is your chance to:

  • Showcase Your Talent: Stand out among peers and demonstrate your skills on a national stage.
  • Compete and Conquer: Test your abilities against some of the brightest minds in the country.
  • Create Lasting Memories: Engage in a vibrant atmosphere filled with camaraderie, competition, and innovation.
Join the Battle! ▶

Prepare yourself for the ultimate tech showdown. Whether you’re a seasoned warrior or a newcomer ready to learn, Prayuddha '25 awaits you. Embrace the challenge, refine your skills, and rise as a tech legend!

Mark your calendars and get ready for an unforgettable experience!
